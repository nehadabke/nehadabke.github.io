You-Choose, a CalHacks project

This fall, my friend Nishali and I created a web application, "you-choose" at CalHacks 5.0

We were inspired by a conversation our friends and I always seem to be having:

"Where do you want to eat?"

"I'm fine with anything, you choose."

"No you choose."

...and repeat.

We thought: how might we break this loop by making something that made an informed decision for us?

From there, we spent the next 48 hours trying to create the answer.
